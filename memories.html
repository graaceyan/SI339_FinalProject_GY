Grace's Favorite Memories

Spring Break 2023, with some of my favorite people ever! I love our friend group so much-- dressing up in all white and taking family photos on the beach will forever be a core memory.

Sophomore retreat with the last beta classes of Alpha Kappa Psi, the business fraternity I'm in! All 30 of us were able to make it to this special weekend in Mears, Michigan, sharing a large cabin and having fun with themed events and 40 mph windy sand dunes!

Blue Mountain 2023! Our large group of 60 spending a rowdy weekend in Blue Mountain, Canada on a ski trip. Trips like these are what little Grace dreamed of back in high school.

My mom and brother came to visit me in Michigan! It was such a fun time introducing them to this portion of my life that they are strangely not a part of. Very nostalgic, as it reminds me that I've grown up.

Pledge retreat for the Beta Omegas! I love my pledge class so much, and I couldn't have asked for a better pledge retreat to conclude a harrowing yet rewarding semester.

I treasure my friends from my hometown so dearly. This ski trip to Lake Tahoe during freshman year Winter Break was so incredibly fun! Distance does make the heart grow fonder.

What is there to say about U-M football. Always so memorable! Go Blue all day everyday!

High school senior trip to Hawaii with my girls! Growing up with strict parents, I thought I'd never be able to go on a vacation with friends until then. These people are the definition of lifelong friends.

High school graduation! What a bittersweet end to an interesting, action-packed 12 years in the Fremont Unified School District. COVID-19, for one, changed a lot but least we had an in-person graduation!
